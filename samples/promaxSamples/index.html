Distinguished Projects

These were presented with awards.

Glee Shout check out the demo:

Interactive unit for Fox's hit show glee. Adobe Creative Suite, animation, coding, lots of fun. The concept was to have the user click on the button to simulate shouting the cast out of the bleachers. There seemed to be a need to make the words and phrases appear more dynamic -- not just fly out of the megaphone one after another. My solution was to have the text fields animate within certain parameters.

Sons of Tucson check out the demo:

The game allows the player to launch the dad character into a pool using a slingshot. The trajectory of the dad's flight is based on power and angle settings the user can adjust in the control panel. If he does not land in the pool, he will hit one of several obstacles. Planning, logic, code, animation - lots of fun!

Human Target check out the demo:

We built this expandable unit for the Fox TV show Human Target. Lot's of planning, coding, animation, sound effects, and interaction tracking. Through some experimentation, I found that 30 pixels on the vertical axis and a random number within a range of 20 pixels on the horizontal access seemed to provide the effect we needed to simulate recoil.
